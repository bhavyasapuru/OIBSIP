Lasanga

Vegetable Lasagna is a delicious and popular Italian dish that is a vegetarian version of traditional lasagna. Instead of using meat, it features layers of pasta, various vegetables, cheese, and a flavorful tomato sauce. Vegetable lasagna is a versatile dish, and you can customize it based on your preferences by adding different vegetables or experimenting with various cheeses and herbs. It's a hearty and satisfying meal that appeals to both vegetarians and those looking for a meatless option.
